awp_cold2 - Sniper War  
Map by: Reilas

Briefing:  
Set in a cold, challenging environment, this  
map offers intense sniper duels. With plenty  
of cover and long-range opportunities, only  
the most skilled snipers will prevail.  

Objective:  
Eliminate the opposing team using your  
sniper rifle. Precision and patience are  
key to winning in this snowy battlefield.  

Have fun and good luck!
