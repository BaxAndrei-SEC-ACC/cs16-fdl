awp_hseyin - Sniper War  
Map by: HSeyiN  

Briefing:  
A sniper’s paradise, this map is designed  
for long-range combat with plenty of elevated  
positions and sightlines. Beware of the dark  
area that adds an element of surprise—stay alert!  
Only the sharpest shooters will reign victorious.  

Objective:  
Eliminate the opposing team with your  
sniper rifle. Use precision, strategy, and  
the environment to your advantage, including  
the dark zone for stealth tactics.  

Have fun and good luck!  

(Press FIRE to continue)
