fy_tuscan - Fight Yard  
Map by: Vova  

Briefing:  
Step into the vibrant streets of Tuscan,  
where fast-paced action awaits. The map's  
narrow paths and open areas provide plenty  
of opportunities for close combat and quick  
decisions.  

Terrorists: Eliminate all Counter-Terrorists.  
Use the environment to outmaneuver and  
surprise your enemies.  

Counter-Terrorists: Take down all Terrorists.  
Control key areas of the map and stay alert  
to secure the victory.
