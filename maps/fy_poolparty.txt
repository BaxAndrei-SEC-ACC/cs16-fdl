fy_poolparty - Fight Yard  
Mapping by: Squall 

Briefing:  
A small recreational pool area has turned  
into a battleground as opposing forces  
clash in a fast-paced skirmish.  

Counter-Terrorists: Eliminate the enemy  
forces. Use the environment for cover and  
strategic positioning to gain the upper hand.  

Terrorists: Take out the opposing team.  
Control the pool area and outmaneuver your  
enemies to secure victory.
