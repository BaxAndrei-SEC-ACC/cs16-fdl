he_glass_2 - Grenade Arena  
Map by: Jepeto  

Briefing:  
This is an enhanced version of the classic  
he_glass map, featuring improved design  
and optimized gameplay for intense battles.  

Objective:  
Up to 20 players can join this chaotic arena.  
Use grenades strategically to shatter the  
glass floors and eliminate your opponents.  

Have fun and may the best player win!  

(Press FIRE to continue)
