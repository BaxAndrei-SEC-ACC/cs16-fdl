de_dust2_snow - Bomb/Defuse  
Map by: ShaLkE  
Original Map by: David Johnston (Creator of de_dust2)  

Briefing:  
Set in a winter wonderland, this snowy version of  
the classic **de_dust2** map brings familiar  
strategic elements with a frosty twist. The snow-covered  
landscape offers new challenges and opportunities.  

Counter-Terrorists: Prevent the Terrorists from  
planting the bomb at one of the two key bomb sites.  

Terrorists: Plant the C4 at either bomb site and  
secure the area until detonation.  

Have fun and good luck!
