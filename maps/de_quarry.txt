Briefing:  
A battle for the environment is underway at a  
quarry site, where terrorists seek to destroy  
machinery that threatens the forest. The clash  
between nature and industry unfolds as two teams  
compete for control.  

Counter-Terrorists: Prevent the Terrorists from  
planting the bomb at the key quarry machinery.  
Defend the equipment and protect the site.  

Terrorists: Infiltrate the quarry and plant the  
C4 to destroy the machines, halting the capitalist  
effort to destroy the forest. Save the forest creatures!
