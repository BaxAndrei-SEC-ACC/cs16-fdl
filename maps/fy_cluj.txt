fy_cluj - Fight Yard  
Map by: Septimius A.I. (Cluj, Romania)  

Release Date: 23/06/2009  

Briefing:  
A thrilling battle set in the heart of Cluj,  
with tight spaces and intense close-quarters  
combat. The map’s layout offers plenty of  
opportunities for strategic plays and quick  
reflexes.  

Objective:  
Eliminate the opposing team using your  
skills and precision. Control key areas of  
the map and make your moves count.  

Enjoy the action and good luck! ;)
