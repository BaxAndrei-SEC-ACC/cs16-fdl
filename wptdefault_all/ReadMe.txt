WarGods CS Waypoint Pack 1.0

----------------
HOW TO INSTALL :
----------------

Simply move all of the .pwf files into your /wptdefault/ folder.

Usually located here :
"cstrike\addons\bot folder\wptdefault\"


Once you have installed all the .pwf files into that folder, your ready to go!
